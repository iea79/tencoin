    Malta is a small island located 80km off of the Southern tip of Italy and boasts mild winters and hot summers, with an annual average temperature of 23°C. Formerly a British colony, Malta is now part of the Eurozone and is classified as an advanced economy by the IMF.

    Malta also ranks highly on the scale set out by the World Economic Forum, which takes into account factors such as employment, healthcare and education.

    Malta is rewriting the rules on cryptocurrencies with their proposal for a new framework which will ensure that the market has legal regulations put in place enabling businesses in the sector to flourish responsibly. As a result of the new measures, a lot of key players on the market move their HQs to Malta.
